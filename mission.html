MISSION

✓ Enhance students' knowledge with technological skills and make them competitive in the world of technology.
✓ Produce students who identify the benefits and harms of technology, avoid the harms and share the necessary benefits.
✓ Integrate the culture, morals, and traditions of the Oromo people with modernized technology.
✓ Introduce the traditions of the Oromo people through innovative technological methods.
✓ Provide hands-on learning experiences, ethical awareness, and encourage entrepreneurship.
        
✓ Promote the Oromo language through digital platforms.
✓ Collaborate with cultural leaders to ensure respect for traditions in technological advancements.
✓ Equip students with entrepreneurial skills to create tech-based businesses.
✓ Ensure students are globally competitive while preserving their unique identity.
✓ Align with Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) to promote quality education and equality.
